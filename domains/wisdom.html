WISDOM

The Foundation of Understanding

Wisdom serves as the philosophical foundation of the Smaily Knowledge Ecosystem. It explores meaning, understanding, insight and human development through a growing body of knowledge and intellectual work.

Domain Overview

The Wisdom Domain investigates the nature of understanding, consciousness, meaning and human insight.

It represents the deepest conceptual layer of the ecosystem and provides the intellectual framework that supports all other domains.

Architecture, Civilization, Research, Thinking and Success are strengthened through the principles developed within Wisdom.
