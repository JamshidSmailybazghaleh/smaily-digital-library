ARCHITECTURE

Space • Form • Geometry • Design

The Architecture Domain explores the principles, structures and mathematical foundations of the built environment.

It connects design, geometry, technical knowledge and human experience within a unified architectural framework.

Domain Overview

Architecture is both a technical discipline and a cultural expression.

Within the Smaily Knowledge Ecosystem, architecture is understood as a bridge between ideas and physical reality.

This domain investigates the relationship between geometry, design, proportion, structure, construction and human life.
