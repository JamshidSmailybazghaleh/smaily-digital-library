CIVILIZATION

Identity • Culture • Heritage • Continuity

The Civilization Domain explores the development, preservation and transformation of human societies, cultures and historical identities.

It examines how communities remember, create meaning and transmit knowledge across generations.

Domain Overview

Civilizations are living systems of memory, knowledge, values and cultural expression.

This domain investigates the historical, cultural and social forces that shape collective identity and long-term development.

It connects heritage, regional studies, historical continuity and cultural resilience within a unified framework.
