Gilan

The Green Jewel of the Caspian

A foundational publication exploring the geography, history, civilization, culture, environment and identity of Gilan as one of the most influential regions of the Caspian world.

Read Sample Digital Library Return Home

Book Overview

Gilan presents a comprehensive exploration of one of the richest cultural and environmental regions of Iran and the southern Caspian basin.

The publication examines the interaction between nature, geography, history, economy, language, culture and civilization, demonstrating how Gilan has played a unique role throughout Iranian history.

Rather than serving merely as a geographical description, this work presents Gilan as a living civilization whose historical continuity continues to shape regional identity and cultural heritage.

Abstract

Gilan explores the geographical, historical, environmental and cultural foundations of one of the most influential regions of the southern Caspian world.

The publication studies the interaction between nature, human settlement, economy, language, identity and civilization while presenting Gilan as a living cultural system shaped through centuries of historical continuity.

This work serves as one of the foundational publications of the Civilization Domain within the Smaily Knowledge Ecosystem.

Author Note

Gilan is far more than a geographical region. It is a historical landscape where civilization, culture, nature and human identity have evolved together across centuries.

This publication seeks to present Gilan as a living intellectual and civilizational system whose legacy extends beyond regional history into the broader heritage of Iranian civilization.
