DOMAIN OVERVIEW

The Architecture Domain represents one of the core pillars of the Smaily Knowledge Ecosystem.

Architecture is understood not merely as a profession, but as an interdisciplinary system connecting geometry, mathematics, design, engineering and human experience.

This domain seeks to reveal the hidden structures that connect form, space, proportion and civilization.
