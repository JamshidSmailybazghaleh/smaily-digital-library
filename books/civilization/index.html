Exploring the foundations of civilization, culture, history and collective memory.

DOMAIN OVERVIEW

The Civilization Domain explores the forces that shape human societies across time.

It examines the relationship between history, identity, culture, geography and collective memory.

Civilizations are sustained not only through institutions and structures, but through shared values, narratives and accumulated knowledge.

This domain seeks to understand how societies emerge, evolve, preserve their heritage and transmit their identity across generations.
