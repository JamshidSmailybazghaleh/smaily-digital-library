ARCHITECTURE OF ARCHITECTURE From Space to Wisdom Foundational Work of the Architecture Domain Overview Architecture of Architecture explores the essence of architecture beyond technical design and construction. The work investigates the relationships between space, meaning, beauty, culture, identity, civilization and wisdom. Purpose To establish a comprehensive conceptual framework for understanding architecture as both a practical discipline and a cultural-intellectual phenomenon. Position within the Ecosystem This book serves as the foundational work of the Architecture Domain. Related architectural publications expand, develop and apply the concepts introduced in this work. Key Themes • Space • Meaning • Beauty • Culture • Identity • Civilization • Wisdom
