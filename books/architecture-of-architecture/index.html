ABOUT THE BOOK

Architecture of Architecture is a foundational work exploring the principles, philosophy, structure and intellectual foundations of architecture.

The book examines architecture not merely as building design, but as a comprehensive system of thought connecting space, form, culture, civilization and human experience.
