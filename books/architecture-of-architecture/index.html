Architecture of Architecture

The Foundational Pillar of the Architecture Domain

ABOUT THE BOOK

Architecture of Architecture is a foundational work exploring the principles, philosophy, structure and intellectual foundations of architecture.

The book examines architecture not merely as building design, but as a comprehensive system of thought connecting space, form, culture, civilization and human experience.

It seeks to establish architecture as an intellectual discipline that extends beyond construction and design, providing a comprehensive framework for understanding the built environment and its relationship to human development.
