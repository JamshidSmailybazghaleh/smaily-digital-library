Architecture of Architecture

The Foundational Pillar of the Architecture Domain

OVERVIEW

Architecture of Architecture is a foundational work examining architecture as a complete intellectual system rather than merely a technical profession.

The book investigates the origins, principles, structure, philosophy and future evolution of architecture while connecting space, form, culture, civilization and human experience.

It aims to establish a comprehensive framework through which architecture can be understood as both a discipline of design and a discipline of thought.

The book is one of the principal pillars of the Smaily Knowledge Ecosystem and serves as the foundation of the Architecture Domain.

AUTHOR NOTE

Architecture is more than construction. It is a language through which civilizations express their values, identities and visions of the future.

This work seeks to reveal the deeper structure behind architectural thought and its relationship with human development.
