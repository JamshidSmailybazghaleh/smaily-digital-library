Book Overview

Architecture of Architecture explores architecture not merely as the design of buildings, but as a comprehensive system of thought connecting space, geometry, culture, civilization, technology and human life.

Abstract

Architecture of Architecture presents a comprehensive theoretical framework for understanding architecture as an integrated discipline that extends beyond the design of buildings.

The book examines architecture as a system connecting space, geometry, engineering, culture, civilization, history, technology and human life. It provides the conceptual foundations upon which future architectural publications within the Smaily Knowledge Ecosystem are developed.
