Mathematics in Architecture

From Point to Infinity

ABOUT THE BOOK

Mathematics in Architecture: From Point to Infinity explores the deep relationship between mathematics, geometry, proportion, structure and architectural creation.

The book investigates how mathematical concepts shape architectural thinking, from the fundamental point and line to complex spatial systems, geometric order and infinite possibilities of form.

Rather than treating mathematics as a purely technical discipline, this work presents it as one of the intellectual foundations of architecture and spatial design.
