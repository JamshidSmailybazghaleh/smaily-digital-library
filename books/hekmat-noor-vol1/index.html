Abstract

Wisdom of Light — Volume I presents the foundational principles of the Light Wisdom System. It explores the relationship between knowledge, consciousness, illumination, human understanding and the architecture of wisdom.

As the first published work of the series, this volume establishes the conceptual framework that will be expanded throughout future volumes of the Wisdom of Light project.
