Phoenix of Deylaman

Civilization, Identity and Historical Continuity

ABOUT THE BOOK

Phoenix of Deylaman explores history, civilization, cultural identity and the enduring continuity of human communities through time.

The book examines the historical and civilizational heritage of Deylaman and its wider significance within Iranian and regional history.
